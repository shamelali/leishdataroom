01 — Problem & Solution

A broken discovery experience.

😩
The Problem
Clients find makeup artists through Instagram DMs, blurry portfolio screenshots, and word-of-mouth. No trusted, centralized platform exists for the client's discovery journey. Guesswork leads to bad bookings and wasted money.
✨
The Solution
Leish is a portfolio-first marketplace where clients browse verified MUA work, read real reviews, and book instantly — no DMs, no guesswork. MUAs get a professional presence and business analytics. Both sides win.
🎯
Why Now
GRWM content is exploding on TikTok. The freelance beauty economy grew post-pandemic. Every major competitor (StyleSeat, Booksy, GlossGenius) focuses on professionals — the client side remains completely wide open.

02 — Market Opportunity

Massive market, underserved entry point.

🌍
Total Addressable Market
The global beauty services market exceeds $200 billion and is growing year-on-year, driven by a surge in freelance beauty professionals and demand for personalized services.
💄
Blue Ocean Positioning
No major competitor dominates TikTok beauty discovery as a booking platform. "MUA near me for [occasion]" is high-intent search with weak marketplace competition. Leish can be first.
📈
Growth Trends
GRWM content exploding on TikTok & Instagram Reels. Consumers increasingly trust portfolio-based hiring. Micro-occasion bookings (graduations, content shoots, birthdays) rising fast.

03 — Business Model

Dual-sided value, multiple revenue streams.

💳
Transaction Commission
A percentage fee on every completed booking. As volume scales, this becomes the primary revenue engine with near-zero marginal cost per transaction.
⭐
MUA Premium Subscriptions
Tiered plans give MUAs enhanced profile visibility, analytics dashboards, "Leish Verified" badge, and priority placement in client search results.
🎯
Featured Placements
Promoted MUA profiles in occasion-specific search results. High-intent, low-competition ad inventory — "Wedding MUAs near you" with real booking intent.
🤝
Brand Partnerships
Beauty brands feature products in MUA portfolios and booking confirmations — natively integrated, non-intrusive format with high engagement rates.

04 — Go-To-Market Strategy

90-day phased launch plan.

Phase 1 · Days 1–30
Awareness
Post 3× per week on TikTok and Instagram Reels with real MUA portfolio transformations tagged "Found on Leish." Launch Facebook community group. Run "Book Your Glow" challenge — zero cost, organic reach.
Phase 2 · Days 31–60
Consideration
Behind-the-scenes MUA vetting content builds trust. Introduce "Leish Verified" badge. Run Facebook/Instagram ads ($200–$300) targeting women 18–38. Hook: "Stop DMing random MUAs on Instagram."
Phase 3 · Days 61–90
Conversion
Activate "First Booking Discount" and free consultation. Retarget website visitors. Launch referral loop — every client who brings a friend earns a perk. Drive first 100 completed bookings.
